Our Helping To The World.

CHRISTYTABLE NONPROFIT is created to help those in need. Our main goal is to create better communities through Togetherness, Empowerment and Unity to create a Greater Generation.

CTF was founded August 21 2019. It is fully registered in Nigeria. Our main focus is building and renovating schools, paying of school fees from grade to university, giving out school supplies, educating both girls and boys about community Empowerment, creating vocational trainings for those who have less or nothing but want to improve their lives, opening of charity shop to help employment of youths and also to support the foundation financially and anyone in need, helping widows, orphans, taking children out of the streets and back to the classroom where where they belong, building boreholes and many more in the rural communities.

OUR MOTTO: "Humanity is our Race, love is our religion," inspires us to serve humanity in these times we live in.

OUR GOAL: Our main goal is to create better communities through Togetherness, Empowerment and Unity for Greater Generation.

Our Vision

To building and renovating schools, paying of school fees from grade to university, giving out sanitary pads to young girls & creating skills acquisition for youths and widows.

Our Mission

Educate out of school children of the importance of education by taking them off the streets back to the classroom where they belong.

We hope to serve humanity by renovating and building schools, building of boreholes in rural areas for clean water, paying of school fees from grade to university level, educating society on the importance of girl child education and hygienic living, taking children out of the streets and back to the classroom where they belong, getting charity shop, creating skills acquisitions such as ( hair braiding, shoe and bag design, coding and many more) for youths and adults including widows in the African nation and nations with similar situations, in the U.S and other nations we hope to battle homelessness by building low income houses for our communities, climate change, building community centers where both youths and adults can come inn for home work help, play games such as chess, basketball, soccer and many more, create skills acquisition such as coding, development of apps, bags and shoe design and many more. We hope to battle hunger by creating the food relief program where people around the globe is fed constantly without dying of hunger.
